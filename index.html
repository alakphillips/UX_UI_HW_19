ABOUT ME

I am a Photographer / UI UX Designer based in Minneapolis, MN. I’ve had the opportunity of working with many large scale projects such as Marten Transport, Bon Iver, Chris Kroeze, and many more. My passion for music & art has shaped my work into it’s current state.


SKILLS

USER RESEARCH

Through user testing and feedback methodologies, I gain valuable insight into the user experience. By conducting interviews and surveys I am able to indetify a user’s concern and develop workable solutions in the prototyping & design phases.

PROTOTYPING

With the information collected in the research phase, I am able to draft a clear concept that meet’s the user’s goals as well as the stakeholders.

UI UX DESIGN

The culmination of this process is carefully designing a final product that enhances usability and creates a well executed user experience.
